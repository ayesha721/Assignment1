Our Services

At Black Tie Event Planner, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet all your event planning needs. Whether you're planning a corporate event, a wedding, or a private party, our experienced team is here to ensure your event is a success.

Our services include:

Event Planning and Coordination

From the initial concept to the final execution, we provide full-service event planning and coordination. We work with you to understand your vision and ensure every detail is perfect.

Venue Selection

Finding the right venue is crucial for the success of any event. We help you select the perfect location that fits your theme, budget, and guest list size.

Catering

Our catering services offer a wide range of culinary options to suit any taste. We work with top chefs and catering companies to provide delicious and beautifully presented food for your event.

Entertainment

From live bands and DJs to performers and speakers, we provide top-tier entertainment to keep your guests engaged and entertained throughout your event.

Decor and Design

Our design team creates stunning décor that transforms any venue into a magical space. We handle everything from floral arrangements and lighting to custom design elements.
